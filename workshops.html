AI Workshops for Businesses

Turn AI Anxiety Into AI Confidence — Safely

  • AI is already entering your workplace.
  • Your employees are experimenting with tools like ChatGPT.
  • Your competitors are exploring automation.
  • Your leadership team knows AI will impact the business.

But most organizations are facing a new and unexpected problem.

  • Employees are already using AI — without training.
  • Sensitive company information can accidentally be shared.
  • Confidential documents can be uploaded to public AI tools.
  • Employees may unknowingly expose proprietary processes or customer data.

At the same time, organizations don't want to ban AI — because the productivity benefits are enormous. The challenge is learning how to use AI safely, responsibly, and effectively.

That's where I come in. I help organizations understand AI, manage the risks, and give teams the confidence to use it productively.

The Hidden Risk Most Companies Are Facing

Right now, in most organizations, employees are secretly experimenting with AI tools to help them work faster — using it for:

  • Writing emails
  • Summarizing documents
  • Brainstorming ideas
  • Analyzing data
  • Generating marketing content

But without training, employees often don't know:

  • What information is safe to share with AI
  • What tools store data or learn from inputs
  • How to protect confidential company information
  • How to avoid exposing intellectual property

Many organizations are already experiencing:

  • Unintentional data exposure
  • Confidential document uploads
  • Internal policy violations
  • Unreliable AI outputs used in business decisions

The goal should not be to block AI. The goal should be to train your team to use it responsibly.
