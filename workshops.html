The Method · A.I.R.

The 3-step method 200+ owners used to go AI-first.

Most owners try to "add AI" on top of a broken workflow — and it flops. A.I.R. flips the order: brief AI first, let it do the work, then you finish. This is the same system we'll install in your business in 30 days.

A

Ask AI first.

For every task, begin with: "Can AI help with this?" or "Could this be done faster or smarter with AI?" This one habit kills manual-first defaults.

I

Implement with AI.

AI handles the draft, the plan, the first pass. Your expertise shapes the result — AI accelerates it. You stop being the bottleneck.

R

Run & refine.

Review, refine, apply your judgment. AI gives you the starting line — you bring the finish. The workflow lives outside your head now.

The 7 questions everyone asks.

Will this work for my industry?+

Yes. The A.I.R. Method is applied on top of your workflows — not a generic industry template. We've trained 200+ owners across coaching, consulting, real estate, wellness, e-commerce, legal, design, and services. If your business sends emails, writes content, or has a repeatable process, this works.

What if I'm not tech-savvy?+

Perfect — this is built for you. If you can use email and a web browser, you can run everything we build. No code. No installs. I translate every technical term into plain English, and we build each workflow with you live in session.

Is the "3 automations or it's free" guarantee really real?+

100%. If we don't ship at least 3 working AI automations into your business by day 30, you get every dollar back — and you keep the templates, prompts, and the A.I.R. playbook. I take the risk. You show up and do the work.

How much time will this take per week?+

About 2 hours of live coaching + 1–2 hours of implementation per week. Total: 3–4 hours for 4 weeks. Most owners say they recover that time by the end of week 2 from the automations we build.

How is this different from an AI course on YouTube?+

Courses teach. This builds. Courses hand you generic prompts. This builds workflows inside your business with your actual documents, voice, and clients. You leave with working systems — not more tabs to read "someday."

What if my team isn't ready for AI?+

That's exactly what the Team Transformation program handles. We install a custom AI policy, train your people together, and give managers a rollout playbook. We start with the 2–3 team members who are curious, and we expand from there.

What happens on the free diagnostic call?+

30 minutes. I ask about your workflows, bottlenecks, and goals. I map your 3 highest-ROI AI opportunities and tell you which program (if any) fits. No pitch-slap. If I don't think we're a fit, I'll say so and point you to the right resource.
