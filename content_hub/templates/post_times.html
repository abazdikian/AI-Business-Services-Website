The YouTube rule no other platform follows

YouTube needs 2–3 hours to index a new upload, run its first test audiences, and build momentum before it recommends the video at scale. So upload 2–3 hours before your audience's peak viewing window — not during it. If your viewers are most active at 7pm, publish at 4–5pm.
