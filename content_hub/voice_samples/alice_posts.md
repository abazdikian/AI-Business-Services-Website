## Voice register notes (for the drafter)

Alice writes in **two distinct registers** — the drafter must pick based on channel:

### Register A — Long-form professional (LinkedIn, Instagram long-caption, YouTube description)
Used in posts 1–6 above.
- **Opener:** declarative stat or contrarian claim. No emoji. Never starts with 🚨/⚡/🔥.
- **Rhythm:** short stanzas, one-line paragraphs, frequent blank lines between ideas.
- **Lists:** arrow bullets `→` for feature/outcome lists. Numbered `1.` `2.` for frameworks.
- **Contrast pattern:** "Fine for X. Not fine when Y." / "AI gave me a template vs. AI helped me build a real plan."
- **Em-dashes** for contrast (`— and it's built for people like us`).
- **Voice markers:** "Here's the thing," "Here's what I teach," "YOU are the bottleneck" (capital YOU for emphasis).
- **CTA:** real Alice CTAs are "Book a free diagnostic — link in profile / link in bio," "DM me," "smallbusinessaicoach.com." Do NOT default to "Reply [word] for the lead magnet" unless the source post uses that pattern — Alice rarely does.
- **Hashtags:** 6–10, mix of branded (`#ClaudeCowork`), niche (`#AIforBusiness`), reach (`#Entrepreneur`, `#SmallBusinessOwner`), personal brand (`#AliceBazdikian` on IG only).

### Register B — Short casual (TikTok, Instagram Reel caption, Short YouTube)
Used in post 7 above.
- **Lowercase** throughout. No capitalization at sentence start.
- **Emojis per line** (one trailing emoji on most lines).
- **Self-deprecating humor** ("questioned my life choices," "tbh").
- **Trending formats:** "things i accomplished while X…" / "the laziest X you'll watch today" / "POV: you tried X."
- **Ultra-short lines**, often 4–8 words.
- **No formal CTA** — caption often ends with a vibe line, not a "Book a call."
- **Hashtags:** 5–7, lowercase-ish, includes platform-native tags like `#TechTok`.
