8 lessons · month one
1
Document everything from day one
Skills, plugins, project structure — live in your CLAUDE.md before you need them.
2
Ask the 95% confidence question — always
Not just at the start. Ask before every major action, not only the first one.
3
Log errors & failures explicitly
So when a script breaks next run, you know why before it costs you time or money.
4
Be explicit about your CLAUDE.md
Tell it exactly what belongs there. It won't guess — you have to say it.
5
Turn repetitive work into Skills early
Every time you repeat a process twice, that's your signal to make it a Skill.
6
Manage your model & token usage
Watch what model you're on and when you're burning tokens unnecessarily.
7
Think about production from the start
Still figuring this one out — but ignoring it early costs you later.
🚧 work in progress
8
Compact your context constantly
Claude rewards it. Run /compact before sessions get heavy — it pays for itself.
