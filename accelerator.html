Questions

Everything you might be wondering.

When exactly does the cohort meet?+
Four Wednesday nights: Jun 10, Jun 17, Jun 24, and Jul 1, from 6–8pm ET. Two hours each, live on Zoom. You will get the calendar invites the moment you reserve your seat.
Why only 6–8 seats?+
Because real implementation needs airtime. With 6–8 owners I can give every business attention on every call. Larger cohorts turn into webinars, and webinars do not change businesses.
How much time do I need outside the live sessions?+
Plan for 1–2 hours of implementation work between sessions. The work pays for itself by week 2 because the workflows you build immediately save time. By week 4, your weekly time-save typically exceeds the implementation hours by 5x.
Do I need technical skills?+
No. If you can use email, Google Docs, and a web browser, you have the full skill set required. No coding, no terminal, no jargon.
What if I cannot make one of the live nights?+
Every session is recorded and available within 24 hours. That said, the rebate requires attending all 4 live, and the live sessions are where the work happens. If you know now you will miss more than one, this cohort is probably not the right one to join.
How is this different from an AI course?+
Courses teach concepts. The Accelerator builds systems. Every session uses your real business as the case study. You leave with working automations running, not just notes you took. Plus live coaching, peer accountability, and a finish-line rebate. None of which exist in a self-paced course.
How exactly does the $100 rebate work?+
Attend all 4 live sessions and submit the implementation work between weeks. After week 4, I refund $100 to your original payment method. That is roughly a 15% rebate on a $697 seat. The intent is simple: I want you to finish, and the rebate is the carrot that makes sure you do.
What happens after I reserve a seat?+
You get a confirmation email, calendar invites for all 4 nights, a one-question intake (so I prep for your business), and a quick check on which community channel you prefer (WhatsApp, Skool, or email). That is it. Nothing else until June 10.
Is there a refund policy beyond the rebate?+
Yes. If you complete week 1 live and decide it is not the right fit before week 2 starts, email me and I refund in full. After week 2 starts, you are committed to the cohort.
What about teams?+
Teams of 3–5 join the same cohort at $550 per seat. We do a 15-minute alignment call before week 1 so the team is rowing in the same direction. Book a team call from the pricing section above.
