Frequently Asked Questions

When does the next cohort start?+
The next cohort starts May 5th, 2026. Cohorts run monthly, starting the first Monday of each month. Spots are capped at 12 per cohort to ensure personal attention.
How much time do I need per week?+
Plan for 3–4 hours per week: one 90-minute live session plus 1–2 hours of implementation work between sessions. The implementation work saves you time immediately, so the net time investment is usually zero by Week 2.
Do I need technical skills?+
Not at all. If you can use email and a web browser, you can do this. Everything is explained in plain language with step-by-step guidance. No coding, no terminal, no jargon.
What if I can't make a live session?+
Every session is recorded and available within 24 hours. You also have access to the private community for async questions. That said, the live sessions are where the magic happens — I'd recommend attending at least 3 of 4.
How is this different from an AI course?+
Courses teach concepts. The Accelerator builds systems. Every session uses YOUR business as the case study. You leave with working automations, not just knowledge. Plus, you have live coaching, peer accountability, and a guarantee — none of which exist in a self-paced course.
What's the guarantee exactly?+
Implement at least 3 AI workflows that save you 5+ hours per week, or get a free 1-on-1 session with Alice to make it happen. For teams: your team will have a working AI policy and at least 5 automated workflows, or the program extends for free. Zero risk.
Can I start with a workshop first?+
You can, but you don't need to. The Accelerator includes everything a workshop covers and goes much deeper. If you're ready to commit to real implementation, the Accelerator is the right starting point. If you just want a taste, book a free 15-minute AI Diagnostic instead.
Is there a payment plan?+
Yes. The individual track is $997 one-time or 2 payments of $547. Team pricing is customized based on team size — book a consultation call to discuss.
