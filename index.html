Mindset Shift

No Matter What Your Role Is, Adopt an AI‑First Mindset

Building an AI-First mindset involves transitioning from a manual-first approach to one where technology is leveraged at the start of a process.

1

Ask the Core Question

For every task, begin by asking, "Can AI help with this?" or "Could this be done faster or smarter with AI?"

2

Let AI Handle the First Draft

Let AI handle the initial step while you focus on finessing the output — your expertise shapes the result, AI accelerates it.

3

Finesse & Own the Output

Review, refine, and apply your judgment. AI gives you the starting line — you bring the finish.

The Core Shift

"Can AI help with this?"

That single question is the foundation of an AI-First mindset. Ask it before every task — meetings, emails, research, planning — and watch how your relationship with work transforms.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Claude Cowork?+
Claude Cowork is a feature inside Anthropic's Claude desktop app that turns AI from a chatbot into a genuine work partner. You point it at a folder on your computer and it can read your files, understand your business context, create documents, and complete complex tasks — no copy-pasting or tech skills required. It starts at $20/month.
Do I need technical skills to work with you?+
Not at all. My workshops and coaching are built specifically for non-technical entrepreneurs, solopreneurs, and business teams. If you can use email and a web browser, you can use Claude. I translate the tech jargon into plain language and walk you through everything step by step.
What happens in the free AI Diagnostic?+
It's a focused 15-minute session where I map your current AI exposure, identify your biggest risks, and surface your most immediate opportunities. You'll walk away with a clear picture of where AI fits in your business and a concrete next step — no pitch, no pressure.
What types of workshops do you offer?+
I offer three formats: a 90-minute hands-on session, a half-day deep dive, and a full-day implementation workshop. All are available in-person (Toronto and Montreal) or virtually. They're built for real employees and leadership teams — practical, jargon-free, and immediately applicable.
How is this different from other AI courses?+
Most AI courses teach you generic prompting techniques. I teach you how to build AI into your actual business workflows — using your real documents, your real clients, your real processes. With 15+ years in business transformation, I focus on practical implementation, not theory. You leave with something working, not just knowledge.
What is the AI Readiness Scorecard?+
It's a free 5-minute quiz that measures where your business stands across AI awareness, data security, employee training, and productivity readiness. You get a personalized score and a recommended next step. No email required to see your results.
