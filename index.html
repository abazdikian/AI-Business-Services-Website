Do I need technical skills?+
Not at all. Everything is built for non-technical entrepreneurs and business teams. If you can use email and a web browser, you can do this. I translate the tech jargon into plain language and walk you through everything step by step.
What is the A.I.R. Method?+
A.I.R. stands for Audit, Implement, Run. It's a 3-phase framework: first we audit your business to find the highest-ROI AI opportunities, then we implement real workflows using your actual processes, then we help you run and scale them with a 90-day roadmap.
Is there a guarantee?+
Yes. Implement at least 3 AI workflows that save you 5+ hours per week, or get a free 1-on-1 session to make it happen. For team programs: your team will have a working AI policy and at least 5 automated workflows, or the program extends for free.
How is this different from AI courses?+
Most AI courses teach you generic prompting techniques you never implement. The Accelerator is live, hands-on, and uses YOUR business as the case study. You leave with working automations, not just knowledge. With 20+ years in business transformation, I focus on practical implementation that sticks.
Can I start with a workshop first?+
Absolutely. Many people start with a 90-minute or half-day workshop, then join the Accelerator for deeper implementation. Workshops are also great for teams that need a taste before committing to the full 30-day program. If you're unsure, book a free AI Diagnostic and I'll recommend the best starting point.
