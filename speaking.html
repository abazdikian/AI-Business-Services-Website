For event organizers

The 8 questions every organizer asks.

What's your speaking fee?+

My fee varies by format, audience size, and travel. Keynotes start at $3,500. Workshop keynotes and half-day trainings start at $5,000. Full-day leadership intensives start at $9,500. Reach out and I'll send a custom quote within 48 hours.

Do you travel? Where are you based?+

I'm based in Toronto, Canada. I travel across Canada and the United States for half-day or larger engagements — travel is billed separately at cost (economy air + one night hotel). For 45-min keynotes I usually prefer virtual unless your event is in Ontario or a flight-hub city.

Can you customize the talk to my audience?+

Always. Every booking includes a 30-minute planning call where we tailor the opening story, industry examples, live-demo use case, and Q&A focus to your specific audience. You're not getting the same canned deck I gave last month.

What tech / AV do you need on site?+

HDMI or USB-C connection to the main screen, a lav mic (preferred) or handheld, reliable internet (I do live AI demos — this matters), a confidence monitor if possible, and a table nearby for my laptop. I bring my own clicker.

What's the guarantee exactly?+

You run a post-event attendee survey (I'll provide the template). If the weighted average rating on "How valuable was this session?" comes in below 4.5/5, you pay nothing. No negotiation. No fine print. I've never had to honor it — but it's real.

How much lead time do you need?+

For a custom keynote: ideally 6–8 weeks. For a workshop or half-day: 8–10 weeks. Can I pull off a 2-week turnaround? Sometimes — but custom content quality drops, so I only recommend it for audiences where my standard talks already fit.

Can we record and reuse the session?+

Yes — internal re-use by your organization or attendees is included. Public distribution (YouTube, podcast clipping, paid replay events) is negotiated separately and usually priced as an add-on to the fee.

What happens after I submit the booking request?+

Within 48 hours you get: availability confirmation, a custom quote, and a short discovery call booked. If we're a fit, a contract lands within another 48 hours. Then we move to the planning call 3–4 weeks before the event to nail the content.
