The Claude Cowork Masterclass:
5 Use Cases That Save 15+ Hours Per Week

35,000+ of you watched my beginner Claude Cowork tutorial. So I recorded a full 1-hour masterclass that goes 3x deeper, with 5 complete use cases you build alongside me.

This one isn't theory. It's a build-along. By the end of the hour, you'll have real AI systems running on your machine — not just knowledge about what AI can do.

Who This Is For

You've heard of Claude Cowork. You've seen the hype. But you haven't built anything with it yet. Or you tried it once, got confused, and closed it. This masterclass takes you from zero to five working systems.

Setup: The Part Where 90% of People Quit

You downloaded Claude Desktop. You opened it. You're staring at a blank screen going "now what?"

This is where most people give up. And I get it — there's no obvious "start here" button.

In the masterclass, I walk through the entire install and setup. What to download, what to click, what to type first. No assumptions about what you already know.

You Walk Away With

Claude Cowork installed, running, and ready on your machine. 10 minutes, zero confusion.

Product Tour: Stop Clicking Random Buttons

You know the feeling of using new software where you keep clicking random buttons hoping to find the right one? Where every feature feels hidden behind three menus you've never seen?

I give you a full walkthrough of the interface. Every button, every panel, every menu. This is the part most tutorials skip because it's "boring." But it's the difference between wasting 3 hours fumbling around and knowing exactly where things are from minute one.

You Walk Away With

A complete mental map of the interface. You'll know where everything lives before you build your first thing.

Skills & Plugins: Where It Gets Serious

Claude Cowork has a plugin system. A plugin is a collection of Skills — reusable instructions that teach Claude how to do something once, so it remembers forever.

No more copy-pasting the same prompts over and over. No more re-explaining your preferences every session.

In the masterclass, I install a marketing plugin that transforms Claude into a content creation machine. Then I create a custom Skill from scratch, live. You follow along and build yours.

"Skills are the section separating people who use Claude casually from people who build systems that save 15+ hours per week."

You Walk Away With

Your first custom Skill installed and working. The foundation for automating anything you do repeatedly.

Use Case 1: Organize Your Local Files

Your Downloads folder has 847 random files in it. Screenshots from last year. PDFs you opened once. Six copies of the same image.

I pointed Claude at my actual messy folder — no fake demo data — and it organized everything in minutes. Photos sorted by date. Documents grouped by type. Duplicates flagged.

Zero manual sorting. Zero dragging and dropping. This is a great first project because it's low stakes, immediately useful, and you see files moving around on your own computer in real time.

You Walk Away With

A clean, organized file system. About 2 hours of manual sorting done in under a minute.

Use Case 2: Personal Email Assistant

You open Gmail. 47 unread. 12 are newsletters. 8 are spam. 3 need replies. And you spend 45 minutes figuring out which is which. Every. Single. Morning.

In the masterclass, I show you how to connect Claude to Gmail and build a personal email assistant. It reads your inbox, summarizes what matters, drafts replies, and flags anything urgent.

The key part: I walk through connecting Gmail via Connectors (MCP) so Claude has real access to your email. Not fake demo data — your actual inbox.

"MCP is what turns AI from a consultant into an employee. Without it, Claude gives you instructions. With it, Claude does the work."

You Walk Away With

A working email assistant connected to your real Gmail. About 45 minutes per day saved on inbox management.

Use Case 3: Create On-Brand Content

This is what I use Claude for every single week. And it's the section where most people say "oh... so this is how she does it."

The problem: you ask AI to write something and it sounds like a robot. Generic. Obviously AI-generated. Your audience can smell it immediately.

The fix is simple: teach Claude everything about your voice and business once. I show you how to build a brand file with your voice, your style, your rules. Claude reads it at the start of every session. It never forgets.

Captions, tweets, newsletters, video scripts — all in your voice. Not generic AI slop. I distribute 250+ pieces of content per week using this exact system. The brand file is the foundation for all of it.

You Walk Away With

Your own brand file + Claude writing content in your voice. The same system behind 250+ weekly posts.

Use Case 4: Create Unlimited Videos

The bottleneck for most creators isn't ideas. It's production. Writing scripts, outlining topics, creating visuals, formatting for different platforms — it takes forever.

I show you how Claude Cowork generates videos using open-source libraries. These work best for animated explainer videos with simple text or motion graphics, though you can incorporate your own assets too.

One person. Unlimited output. The solopreneur dream.

You Walk Away With

A repeatable video production workflow. Go from "I have an idea" to "I have a script + outline + visuals" in minutes, not hours.

Use Case 5: Social Media Content Calendar

"What should I post today?" If you're asking this question every morning, you've already lost.

The best creators never wonder what to post. They have a system.

I show you how to build a full content calendar with Claude. Plan what you're posting, where, and when. Distribute seamlessly across multiple platforms. Claude brainstorms topics, batches content, schedules posts, and keeps everything organized in one place.

No more panic posting. No more going dark for two weeks because life got busy.

You Walk Away With

A full content calendar across multiple platforms. A system that runs even when you don't feel like creating.
