My Come-to-Jesus Moment with AI

March 16, 2026 · 5 min read · Personal Essay

I have been following AI news, newsletters, platforms, tools, YouTube influencers, and SKOOL communities for more than a year now. I didn't engage beyond prompting ChatGPT and Claude to do some cool things, yes, time saved writing contracts, market researching, and content creation, but as a SaaS project manager in a previous life, I knew that vibe-coding might be fun, but actually deploying a functioning workflow that doesn't break and keeps working after I'm done is a whole different story. I had no interest in spending time creating toys that would break sooner or later.

The Observer Phase

I did, however, start teaching AI to entrepreneurs for market research, business plan creation, and marketing. And I loved every time I hit enter on a prompt that delivered a punch of information to an audience who always asked me: "Where do I start? Which AI tool do I use?"

Seeing their feedback and reactions was extremely satisfying because I knew I was delivering huge value with only a few prompts and zero tech skills required from my audience.

The Teaching Moment

And then last month, when I ran an AI session for small businesses, I saw the light at the end of the tunnel for entrepreneurs and businesses while running a live session with Claude. Midway through the session, one of the attendees exclaimed:

"You have converted me to Claude!"

That moment confirmed everything I had been feeling. This wasn't just another AI tool. This was something fundamentally different.

The Breakthrough

Looking back at my six years of experience in Business Intelligence at the biggest brewer in the world, as a Business Analyst who stitched ten data sources together to report daily on sales and market share, or my years as an entrepreneur running every function in our two-person startup, Claude Cowork is what I have been waiting for this whole time.

And now, after just a few weeks of working with it, I already know all the ways I would use it to save time daily, either as a business analyst or an entrepreneur.

Claude Code Changed Everything

And then comes Claude Code. No tech skills necessary. A weekend later, I have two gorgeous websites created with multiple funnels waiting to be activated. I have a small tech stack, all free except Claude. Yes, you do have to subscribe to take advantage of Claude Code and Cowork, the best $20/month you will ever spend. But if you are like me and you see the endless automation possibilities, you will quickly upgrade to $100/month and have a full-time assistant, coder, and business analyst at your fingertips.

The Invitation

I haven't been this excited about AI, but now we are truly at the intersection of AI and business value at every level: from a solopreneur, to a small team, to a big company. With Claude, there are hundreds of ways to save time. All we need is a workflow to start with.
