Questions

The stuff you're probably wondering.

Do I need to already have Claude or know how to use it?
No — you just need to be curious. We'll cover the setup from the beginning. If you've been using Claude casually, you'll walk away with a completely different relationship with it.
Is this actually free? What's the catch?
It's genuinely free. I do workshops and coaching for a living, so at the end I'll mention ways to work with me more deeply. But the webinar delivers complete value on its own. You don't need to buy anything to get results.
What does "Claude Cowork" mean exactly?
It's the strategy of setting Claude up as an ongoing business collaborator — not just a one-off tool. We use Claude's Projects feature, persistent memory, and custom context to build something that actually understands your business over time.
What if I can't make it live?
A replay will be available for everyone who registers. You'll receive it within 24 hours. Register regardless of whether you can make the live time — you won't miss the content.
Is this relevant if my business isn't in tech?
Especially relevant. This is built for consultants, coaches, creatives, service providers, and retailers — not software companies. Every example is designed for businesses that sell expertise, services, or products.
I'm neurodivergent. Will this actually be useful for me?
Yes — explicitly. There's an entire section on how neurodivergent entrepreneurs can configure Claude to work with their cognitive style. The goal is more clarity, less overwhelm, and systems that feel aligned.
I tried ChatGPT and it didn't really work for my business.
That's the most common thing I hear — and it's not your fault. ChatGPT out of the box is generic. Claude Cowork is different because we configure it specifically for your business, your voice, and your actual workflows. That setup step is exactly what this webinar is about.
Will AI make my content sound robotic or like everyone else?
No — and this is a core principle of what I teach. We train Claude on your past content, your language patterns, and your brand voice so the output sounds like you on your best day, not a generic template. Your clients hire you. We keep it that way.
I don't have time to learn a new system right now.
That's exactly why this webinar exists. You're not adding a new learning project — you're getting time back. Most participants have their first workflow running before we're even done. The goal is for you to leave with hours saved, not more homework.
Is this for solopreneurs or is it built for corporate teams?
Both — and I'll be explicit about which strategies apply to you throughout the session. Solo founders, women entrepreneurs, coaches, creatives, and consultants make up the majority of attendees. Managers and small team leaders are also well served. You'll know clearly which frameworks are designed for your situation.
